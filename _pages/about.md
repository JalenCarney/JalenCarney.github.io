I am an aspiring game programmer with a strong foundation in Unity development and a 
growing skill set that extends into 3D modeling, texturing, UVing and animating in Blender, video 
editing with Adobe and sprite creation in Aseprite. I am someone who enjoys bringing ideas to 
life through interactive experiences and as a lifelong gamer, pursuing game development felt 
like the most natural path for me to take and I’m even more so sure of that now. I am motivated 
by the idea of crafting a lasting impression, experiences that players can look back on with fond 
memories and think of as “the good ol’ days”.

My design philosophy has always been rooted in hands-on experimentation. I typically generate 
ideas based on what excites me and whether it’s something I have never implemented before or 
a concept I want to explore more deeply. I prefer to learn by doing, building projects that push 
me to develop new skills. My primary workflow involves Unity for programming and 
implementation, while Blender and Aseprite have allowed me to expand into visual design. In 
my projects, I often challenge myself to integrate both technical systems and visual elements to 
ensure that what I make is something original by me.

Looking ahead, I aspire to build a career in the game industry where I can contribute 
meaningfully to the industry and grow as a true creator. I know that I have the drive to 
continuously improve across multiple disciplines and ultimately, I hope to create the type of 
experiences that resonate with people and establish a legacy of work that people will remember 
far into the future.
